The Challenge

Built specifically to solve the complex financial forensics problem statement. Traditional rule-based AML systems are fundamentally flawed as they analyze transactions in isolation.

  • Overwhelming high false positive rates.
  • Inability to detect cross-border network fraud.
  • Failure to spot deep cyclical laundering.

Our Graph-Based Solution

SecureNet maps transaction relationships as multi-dimensional graphs, easily uncovering hidden laundering structures (smurfing, circular routing) that traditional SQL engines miss entirely.

Frequently Asked Questions

AML SecureNet uses a local WebAssembly engine. When you upload a CSV, it is parsed and mapped entirely within your browser's memory. No transaction data is ever sent to our servers.

The client-side engine can comfortably process files up to 2GB (approx. 10 million transactions) depending on the available RAM on your local workstation.

Instead of rigid rules, we use graph-clustering algorithms that score the "neighborhood" of a transaction. A node only triggers an alert if its surrounding network structure matches known laundering topologies.
